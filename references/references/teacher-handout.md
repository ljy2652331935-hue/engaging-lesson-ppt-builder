# Teacher Handout And Student Worksheet Reference

Use this when the user asks for teacher讲义, lesson notes, printable materials, or student worksheets.

## Teacher Handout Shape

The teacher handout should be a backstage guide, not a PPT transcript.

Recommended sections:

1. Lesson goal in one sentence.
2. 40-50 minute flow with rough timing.
3. Slide-by-slide teaching cards.
4. Misconception guardrails.
5. Board plan.
6. Exit ticket answers and scoring.
7. Optional extension questions.

## Slide Teaching Card

For each slide or slide pair, include:

- Slide number and title.
- Learning job.
- First teacher question.
- Likely student answers.
- Landing sentence.
- Misconception warning.
- Optional board note.

Keep each card short enough for a teacher to glance at during class.

## Student Worksheet Shape

A one-page student task sheet should include:

- Hook prediction.
- One observation or drawing task.
- One short calculation or reasoning task.
- One "explain to a younger student" question.
- Exit ticket area.

Do not reproduce all slide content. The worksheet should make students do something.

## Scoring Rubric

Use simple classroom scoring:

- Concept accuracy.
- Evidence or reasoning.
- Clear language.
- Correct use of key vocabulary.

For science topics, include "acceptable answer" and "common wrong answer" so the teacher can grade quickly.
